About ButwalCitizen

Built for
the People of Butwal.

ButwalCitizen is an initiative by Butwal Metropolitan City to digitize and simplify civic services for every resident across all 12 wards.

  • Transparent Governance — Every complaint is tracked publicly, with timelines and responsible officers named.
  • Accessible to All — Nepali and English language support. Works on any device, including feature phones via USSD.
  • Ward-Level Precision — All 12 wards of Butwal Sub-Metropolitan have dedicated digital offices and ward-specific services.
  • Real-Time Updates — SMS and app notifications keep citizens informed on complaint status, bill dues, and city notices.
